# Connect Canvas Vision

Connect Canvas helps people notice what is happening between them and move toward clearer shared understanding.

The product should feel calm, respectful, and lightweight. It should invite reflection without pretending to diagnose, score, or fix a relationship.

## Product north star

Create a private, mobile-first space where two people can explore patterns, name differences, and find a constructive next conversation.

## Current scope

S1-001 establishes the application foundation only. Business flows, Discovery experiences, matching logic, and user-facing relationship features are intentionally deferred.
